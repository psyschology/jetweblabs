One-Time Setup Fee

This covers the initial strategy, design, and development of your chatbot flow. We'll map out customer journeys, write the conversation scripts, and build the core automation logic. This is a one-off investment to get you started.

Monthly Platform Fee

This is a recurring fee for the software that powers your chatbot. It includes hosting, access to the automation builder, platform updates, and basic support. The price varies based on the features you need (see Feature Tiers below).

Meta's Conversation Fees

Meta (owner of WhatsApp) charges a small fee per conversation after your first 1,000 free service conversations each month. This is a direct cost from Meta and depends on your usage. We explain this in detail further down.

Optional: Monthly Maintenance & Optimization

For businesses that want to continuously improve. This optional retainer includes monthly performance reports, chatbot flow optimizations, updating menus/promos, and priority support.
