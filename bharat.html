Built for Scale, Security, and Resilience

Our proposed architecture is based on modern, cloud-native principles to ensure the platform is performant, scalable, and easy to maintain. This section outlines the robust technical foundation of Project BharatConnect.

An Impenetrable Fortress for National Data

Security is not an afterthought; it is the bedrock of Project BharatConnect. We propose a multi-layered security strategy that protects data at every stage, ensuring compliance with India's IT Act and data protection laws.

End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

All communications on Varta are secured with the Signal Protocol, ensuring only the sender and recipient can read messages.

Data-in-Transit & At-Rest Encryption

Using TLS 1.3 for data in transit and AES-256 for data at rest, all platform data is encrypted at all times.

Zero Trust Architecture

Every request is authenticated and authorized, regardless of its origin, minimizing internal and external threats.

Regulatory Compliance

Designed from the ground up to comply with the Indian IT Act, DPDP Act, and other relevant government regulations.

AI-Powered Moderation

Advanced AI/ML models for Sandesh to detect and flag hate speech, misinformation, and harmful content in real-time.

Regular Security Audits

Continuous VAPT and third-party audits by CERT-In empanelled agencies to ensure the platform's security posture is robust.
