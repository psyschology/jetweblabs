Frequently Asked Questions

What services does JetWeb Labs offer?

JetWeb Labs offers a wide range of web development services including custom website development, UI/UX design, e-commerce integration, web app development, SEO optimization, and ongoing maintenance and support.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take several months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support and maintenance packages to ensure your website continues to perform optimally. This includes regular updates, security patches, and technical support.

What is your pricing model?

We offer both fixed-price project-based pricing and hourly rates depending on the project's nature. We provide a detailed, transparent quote after the initial requirements gathering session.
